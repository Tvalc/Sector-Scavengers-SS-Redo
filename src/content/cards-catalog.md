## KEY TERMS

- **Scrap**: Salvage value collected during a dive
- **Comp/Component**: Ship hardware components found in salvage
- **Relic**: Special high-value salvage items
- **Echo**: Meta-currency carrying between runs (from Void mechanics)
- **Shld/Shield**: Temporary hit points that negate damage
- **Dng/Danger**: The damage/effect you take each round
- **Breach**: Hull damage taken when extracting
- **Rnd/Round**: One cycle of dive play
- **Debt**: The monetary burden carried between expeditions
- **Overcharge**: Spend +1⚡ for enhanced effects
- **Refund**: Get energy back when condition is met
- **Surge**: Card that gives energy instead of costing
